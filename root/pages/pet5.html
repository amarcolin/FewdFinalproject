About Face

Face was found as a stray with untreated demodex mange. That is why he is spotted with hairless and missing his hair on his face, neck and chest area. This hair will grow back and this type of mange is not contagious. He is undergoing treatment and takes a medication daily. He needs to stay on this medication until he has 2 negative skin scrapings. This may take several months so his adopter needs to be willing to put in the time to wait for this handsome boy to look the wayscraping.

Face got his name after the character on the A Team. Some are too old or too young to know him but this guy is totally Face. He has a confidence about himself and struts down the sidewalk when he walks. He loves playing fetch, talking to people and carrying his toys around. He is getting the hang of crate training but it is a work in progress.

He does well with dogs and cats and loves truck rides.

Southern Maryland has a large population of stray pets who need their forever homes.
