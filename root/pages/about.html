Animal Relief Fund

ARF is a non-profit organization dedicated to finding homes for abandoned animals. We are a small group of people in Southern Maryland who are trying to make a difference for the animals.

Our missions are:

  • To provide rescue and adoption for dogs, cats, kittens, puppies, and occasionally other small and furry animals.
  • To provide information to the community on low cost spay/neuter programs and veterinary care, pet care, house/crate training, and animal behavior/training. Our hope is that our efforts will help decrease the pet overpopulation problem that is caused by lack of spay/neutering, and relinquishment/abandonment of pets.
  • We take in ABANDONED animals, see that they are healthy and ADOPTED, and that they are ADORED for life by their adopters and the ARF organization.

We are NOT a shelter, and our animals are housed in our homes. We have many wonderful animals in foster care right now looking for permanent loving homes. Please stop by our adoption days and meet them.

Days are held every Saturday at the Well Pet Clinic in Lexington Park, MD from 11am to 2pm. The Well Pet Clinic is located at 21800 North Shangri La Dr # 16 in Lexington Park.
