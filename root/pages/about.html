Animal Relief Fund

What is ARF?

Animal Relief Fund is a non-profit organization dedicated to finding homes for abandoned animals. We are a small group of people in Southern Maryland who are trying to make a difference for the animals.

What is our mission?

  • We provide rescue and adoption for dogs, cats, kittens, puppies, and occasionally other small and furry animals.
  • We provide information to the community on low cost spay/neuter programs and veterinary care, pet care, house/crate training, and animal behavior/training. Our hope is that our efforts will help decrease the pet overpopulation problem that is caused by lack of spay/neutering, and relinquishment/abandonment of pets.
  • We take in ABANDONED animals, see that they are healthy and ADOPTED, and that they are ADORED for life by their adopters and the ARF organization.

How does it work?

We are NOT a shelter, and our animals are housed in our homes. We have many wonderful animals in foster care right now looking for permanent loving homes.

Please meet them on our ADOPTION DAYS: every Saturday from 11am to 2pm at the Well Pet Clinic, North Shangri La Dr # 16 in Lexington Park, MD.
